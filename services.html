Services

I currently offer two paid services: Video Analysis and Video Chat.

Video Analysis

Introduction

“Not everybody is lucky enough to have access to an on-court coach.”

This service makes it easy to have your game analyzed, both technically AND tactically. All from the comfort of your home! You record yourself playing, send it to me and I analyze it and send a video back, showing how to improve.

When you are ready to start or if you have any questions, email me: phillip@bettersquash.com

Video Chat

Want to talk about your squash performance or your training? In fact, we can discuss anything related to squash you want.

I have found that a 20-minute video chat is the right amount of time for most people.

You decide what we chat about, so please prepare some questions or talking points.

I don’t need any videos of your playing - this is an opportunity for you to chat about squash, not an analysis of your playing.

The cost is 15 Euros, which includes an email summary of your action points (if appropriate).

This could be a perfect gift for a squash playing family-member, friend or colleague.

Schedule a chat now

Still Have Questions?

If you have any questions or doubts, can't find a suitable date/time, or just require more information, please send an email to videochat@bettersquash.com and I'll reply within 24 hours.
